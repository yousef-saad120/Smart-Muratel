About the Research Chair

The Research Chair of Online Dialogue and Cultural Communication is a pioneering initiative dedicated to advancing meaningful digital interactions and cross-cultural understanding. Led by Dr. Mejdl Safran, the chair focuses on innovative projects that bridge technology and dialogue for societal impact.
